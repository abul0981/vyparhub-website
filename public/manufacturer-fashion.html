❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to common questions
How to upload my first product? ▼
Go to "Upload Product" in the sidebar. Follow the AI-guided upload flow — add photos, and our AI will auto-suggest colors, categories, and tags. It takes less than 5 minutes!
How do I get GST verified badge? ▼
Go to Settings → Business Verification. Enter your GST number and upload your GST certificate. Verification takes 24–48 hours. Once verified, you'll see the ✅ GST Verified badge on your profile.
When do I receive payment for orders? ▼
Payments are credited to your registered bank account within 7 working days of successful delivery confirmation. You can track payment status in the Orders section.
How to handle a return request? ▼
Go to Returns & Disputes section. Review the return case, upload packing proof video if needed, and choose to Approve or Reject. If you suspect fraud, use the "Raise Fraud Ticket" option.
How do VyparHub Ads work? ▼
VyparHub Ads allow you to promote products directly to retailers browsing the platform. You set daily budget, target states and retailer types. AI recommends best products and budgets for maximum ROI.
